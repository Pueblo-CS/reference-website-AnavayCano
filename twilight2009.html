Bella Swan is soon to be 18 and still happy with her undead boyfriend, Edward Cullen. At her birthday gathering with the Cullens they experience a terrible incident that shows Edward the true dangers of their romance. He soon deciedes to leave Forks to protect Bella, costing them their relationship - leaving her angry, depressed and confused. While he's gone Bella puts herself in many dangerous situations to try and bring Edward back to Forks until he's convinced shes dead. Edward goes through a state of depression until him and his sweetheart are reunited. Where the Vultori soon becomes an issue in their romance.
